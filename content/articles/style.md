## Tables

Normal tables:

| Name  | Age |
| ---   | --- |
| Bob   | 27  |
| Alice | 23  |

Inline Markdown within tables:

| Inline    | Markdown | In                | Table  |

## List Types

Ordered list:

1. First item
1. Second item
1. Third item

Unordered list:

* List item
* Another item
* And another item

Nested list:

* Item
  1. First Sub-item
  1. Second Sub-item
